# üì± How to Install CKC Clinic System on Your Mobile Phone

The CKC Clinic System is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** that can be installed on your phone like a regular app from the App Store or Google Play.

---

## üì± For Android Phones (Chrome Browser)

### Step 1: Open the Website
1. Open **Google Chrome** browser on your Android phone
2. Visit the clinic system URL (e.g., `https://ckc-clinic.com` or your server address)
3. Wait for the page to fully load

### Step 2: Install the App
You'll see **one of these options**:

#### Option A: Automatic Install Prompt
- A pop-up banner will appear at the bottom saying **"Add CKC Clinic to Home screen"**
- Tap **"Install"** or **"Add"**
- The app will be added to your home screen

#### Option B: Manual Installation
1. Tap the **three dots menu** (‚ãÆ) in the top-right corner
2. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
3. You'll see a confirmation popup with the app name and icon
4. Tap **"Add"** or **"Install"**
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen

### Step 3: Launch the App
- Find the **"CKC Clinic"** icon on your home screen
- Tap it to open the app
- It will open in full-screen mode, just like a native app!

---

## üçé For iPhone/iPad (Safari Browser)

### Step 1: Open the Website
1. Open **Safari** browser (must be Safari, not Chrome)
2. Visit the clinic system URL
3. Wait for the page to fully load

### Step 2: Install the App
1. Tap the **Share button** (‚ñ° with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
2. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. You'll see a preview with the app name **"CKC Clinic"** and icon
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top-right corner

### Step 3: Launch the App
- Find the **"CKC Clinic"** icon on your home screen
- Tap it to open the app
- It will open like a regular iOS app!

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### "Add to Home Screen" option not showing
- **Make sure** you're using Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone)
- **Check** that the website is using HTTPS (secure connection)
- Try **refreshing** the page
- Make sure you're **not in Incognito/Private mode**

### App not opening in full screen
- **Uninstall** the app from home screen
- **Clear** browser cache
- **Reinstall** following the steps above

### Can't find the app icon
- Check **all home screen pages**
- Look in the **app drawer** (Android)
- Search for "CKC Clinic" in your phone
